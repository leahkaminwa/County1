    Governor’s Welcome Note

    I salute the people of Nairobi and the Nation of Kenya. And welcome you to Nairobi County. ‘The City Under the Sun’. county has unique features, challenges and opportunities which we vowed to take up and tackle. Mahatma Gandhi Said “the greatness of a nation can be judged by the way its animals are treated”.

    Our resolve remains to bring together all the available resources, identify the opportunities and purposefully take an integrated approach to have all policies programmes and activities in the county aligned towards raising the welfare and sustainable quality of life and healing our society, people and ecosystems.
